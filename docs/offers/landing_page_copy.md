## Hero

### Headline

Offline-first bug bounty operations preparation without unsafe automation.

### Subheadline

`bugos` helps authorized researchers and teams structure redacted program briefs, scope blockers, evidence hygiene, report templates, and final human-review gates. It does not scan, exploit, contact targets, handle credentials, or submit reports.

### CTA

Request a prepare-only intake review.

## What bugos Does

- Turns a redacted program brief into a structured intake pack.
- Highlights blockers before manual work begins.
- Creates evidence and report templates.
- Flags unknown scope, known-issue, and duplicate-risk areas.
- Keeps final decisions conservative: `BLOCK` or `NEEDS_HUMAN_REVIEW`.
- Keeps automatic submission disabled.

## What bugos Does Not Do

- No scanning.
- No exploitation.
- No payload generation.
- No target interaction.
- No credential handling.
- No password-reset testing.
- No browser automation.
- No crawling.
- No automatic submission.
- No guaranteed bounty outcomes.

## Who It Is For

- Authorized bug bounty researchers.
- Small security teams.
- Operators who want cleaner scope and evidence workflows.
- Teams that need local templates before human review.

## Deliverables

- Program profile
- Disqualifier check
- Known-issue map
- Program score
- Work order
- Manual test cards
- Evidence manifest template
- Report draft template
- Final submission check

## Safety-First Promise

Every output is prepare-only unless a qualified human reviewer confirms authorization, scope, evidence rules, and readiness. Even then, `bugos` does not automate testing or submission.

## FAQ

### Does bugos hack targets?

No. It does not contact targets, scan, exploit, crawl, or run payloads.

### Does bugos submit bug bounty reports?

No. Automatic submission remains disabled. Any submission must be manual and outside the prepare-only workflow.

### Can bugos guarantee bounty payouts?

No. It does not guarantee valid findings, accepted reports, or income.

### Can I send real credentials or private data?

No. Remove secrets, tokens, credentials, cookies, session data, payment data, classroom data, and third-party personal data before using any workflow.

### What is the main benefit?

Cleaner pre-work: scope clarity, blocker detection, evidence hygiene, and report-readiness structure before a human decides what to do next.
