# bugos Pricing Hypothesis

Status: draft for human review

This document implements REV-009 from `docs/revenue_backlog.md`. It contains pricing hypotheses only. It is not a payment page, invoice, financial advice, tax advice, legal advice, or guarantee of revenue, payout, accepted findings, or customer demand.

## Pricing Principles

- Price only prepare-only work.
- Do not price target testing, scanning, exploitation, payload generation, credential handling, password-reset testing, browser automation, crawling, or submission.
- Keep automatic submission disabled.
- Use clear deliverables and boundaries.
- Avoid success fees or bounty-share claims until reviewed legally and commercially.
- Do not guarantee valid findings, accepted reports, payouts, revenue, or outcomes.

## Validation Questions

Before using prices externally, review:

- Is the buyer clearly B2B or consumer?
- Are taxes and invoicing handled correctly?
- Are refund and delivery terms clear?
- Are data-handling responsibilities clear?
- Is the offer clearly prepare-only?
- Are all guarantees excluded?
- Are payment terms reviewed?
- Are legal and privacy documents ready?

## No-Go Pricing Models Without Review

Do not use without qualified review:

- bounty percentage model
- success fee model
- subscription with unclear deliverables
- retainer with unclear data handling
- automated report pipeline pricing
- target-testing package pricing
- scanner or exploit-service pricing

## Suggested First Test

Use a manual, low-risk validation approach only:

1. Share synthetic demo pack manually with a known appropriate reviewer.
2. Ask whether the prepare-only output is useful.
3. Ask which artifact has the most value.
4. Ask whether pricing ranges feel reasonable.
5. Do not collect payment until terms, privacy, invoicing, and delivery boundaries are reviewed.

## Stop Conditions

Stop pricing work if the next step requires:

- payment collection
- invoicing
- tax handling
- external publication
- success-fee promises
- income claims
- target interaction
- scanner, exploit, payload, credential, or submission services
