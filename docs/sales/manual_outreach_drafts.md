## FAQ Reply: Is this a scanner?

No. `bugos` does not scan, crawl, exploit, run payloads, contact targets, or automate browser activity. It prepares local documentation and review artifacts from redacted material.

## FAQ Reply: Does this submit reports?

No. Automatic submission remains disabled. Any submission decision must be manual, separately authorized, and outside the prepare-only workflow.

## FAQ Reply: Can you guarantee bounties or valid findings?

No. There is no guarantee of valid findings, accepted reports, payout, revenue, or any specific outcome.

## FAQ Reply: What should I send?

Only redacted material. Do not send credentials, tokens, cookies, session data, personal data, payment data, classroom data, private account content, or third-party private data.

## FAQ Reply: What happens after I send a redacted brief?

The output is a prepare-only intake pack. It clarifies blockers, scope assumptions, evidence expectations, report structure, and whether the next step remains blocked or needs human review.
